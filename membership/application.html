Become a Chi Hack Night Member

Welcome to Chi Hack Night's Membership Application form.

Here you can sign up to apply to become a Chi Hack Night member! Members are eligible to run for the Chi Hack Night Board of Directors and vote in board elections.

These are the requirements to join Chi Hack Night as a member:

  1. Fill out this application
  2. Fulfill membership requirements and maintain them on an annual basis (one of the following)
    1. Attend at least 8 Chi Hack Night events per year and pay a $50 annual membership fee, or
    2. Attend at least 8 Chi Hack Night events and volunteer for Chi Hack Night for 4 or more hours per year, or
    3. Attend at least 15 Chi Hack Night events per year
  3. Agree to abide by the Code of Conduct and other Chi Hack Night Member policies as determined by the board and posted at Chi Hack Night Policies
    • Code of Conduct
    • Bylaws
